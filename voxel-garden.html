🏝️ Voxel Isle

A tiny floating island, all yours. Grow a garden in the sky.

  • Drag to orbit · scroll / pinch to zoom · Q / E rotate
  • ⚒️ Till grass into soil, 🌱 plant a seed, 💧 water it (grows 2× faster)
  • Tap a ripe plant (it sparkles) to harvest it for coins & XP
  • 🧱 Build with blocks & decorations · ⛏️ Dig to remove
  • Level up to unlock new seeds, decor — and new floating islets
  • Moonpetals only grow at night. The stars are good for something. 🌙
  • Seasons turn every 3 days — spring speeds growth, winter slows it and brings snow ❄️
  • Keys 1–5 pick tools · M mute · P photo mode · 🏆 achievements · autosaves
