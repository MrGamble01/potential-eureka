How it plays

Hire engineers to ship features at desks. Each feature routes to your CEO or a VP who multiplies the deal.

On the sales side, BDRs solo small deals and hand off larger ones to AEs for a 3× close.

Keep 🥫 Zyn and 💊 Adderall stocked — engineers burn a Zyn per feature and self-serve from the Adderall Cabinet for a speed amp. Let a desk run dry and shipping stalls.

Hit the season's revenue goal, then IPO for 2× deal value and a tougher next round. Achievements along the way.
